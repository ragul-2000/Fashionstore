Help Center

How do I create an account?
To create an account on our website, follow these steps:
1. Click on the "Sign Up" or "Create Account" button.
2. Fill in the required information, such as your name, email, and password.
3. Click on the "Submit" button to create your account.
You will receive a confirmation email once your account is successfully created.
How can I track my order?
To track your order, follow these steps:
1. Log in to your account.
2. Go to the "Orders" section in your account dashboard.
3. Find the order you want to track and click on the "Track Order" button.
You will be able to see the real-time status and updates of your order.
